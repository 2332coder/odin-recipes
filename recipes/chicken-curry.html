chicken curry recipe

Chicken curry is a flavorful and versatile dish enjoyed in many cuisines worldwide, particularly in South Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Caribbean. It typically features chicken cooked in a spiced sauce, often with a base of onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut Milk or Yogurt

How to Make chicken curry Step-By-Step

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  2. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook into a thick sauce.
  4. Stir in the marinated chicken and cook through.
  5. Add coconut milk or yogurt for creaminess.
  6. Simmer until flavors combine and chicken is tender.
